## Digital Detox Weaver: Data Visualization Design

**Design Philosophy Reinforcement:**
*   **Accessibility First:** WCAG AA compliance (4.5:1 contrast, colorblind-friendly palettes, keyboard navigation, descriptive alt-text).
*   **Information Hierarchy:** Clear visual flow, primary insights prominent, supporting details on demand.
*   **Interactive Discovery:** Empower users to explore, filter, and drill down into data.
*   **Mobile-Responsive:** Layouts and interactions designed to adapt gracefully to various screen sizes.
*   **Healthcare Context:** Emphasize actionable insights, avoid alarmist
